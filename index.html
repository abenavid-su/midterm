Copenhagen

If there’s a reason why the Danish are said to be the happiest people on Earth, it’s definitely because of Copenhagen. Not only is Denmark the source of various storybook fairytales... but it is one itself. This eco-friendly city is the hub of innovation and buzzing with energy no matter where you go. Whether you’re into art, outdoor activities, or culture, you are sure to find something to your liking.
